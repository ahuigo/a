### url_for
url_for 只能用于启动时的context, 用来确定url:
1. abc 函数的访问url
2. 静态地址的访问url: 

    print(url_for('static', filename='stylex.css')) # static 是关键字
    with app.test_request_context('/a/b/c', method=['POST']):
        print(url_for('abc',a=1,b=2)); # /?a=1&b=2
